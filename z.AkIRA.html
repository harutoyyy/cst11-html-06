Akira Toriyama

(April 5, 1955 - March 1, 2024) was a Japanese manga artist and designer. His production company is BIRD STUDIO. Born in Nagoya City, Aichi Prefecture and currently resides in Kiyosu City (formerly Kiyosu Town area). Blood type A. His wife is Mika Minachi, a shoujo manga artist. He got popular around the world through his comics
